SPEARED V2.0

SPEARED is a framework for developing programs for robots in an Augmented Reality(AR) environment. The framework was first developed by Michael Wieneke and Jonas Eilers during their Master’s thesis and the aim of the framework is to make the robot development experience very similar to what is obtainable in the real world production scenario.
The framework allows that the debugging experience of the robot program occurs in a simulated environment that is linked to reality and this ensures that the actions of the robot is seen to be very close to reality.
One interesting property that we look to exploit in the SPEARED V2.0 is the robot programming by demonstration through the use of gestures and voice commands.This should allow users who are not programmers to make use of robots for their daily activities.
