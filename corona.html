Working in Times of Corona

Corona has stalled many aspects of daily life. However, nothing stopped our amazing team and we worked on this project on a continuous basis. It was rather a joy to meet virtually and decide interesting points in our project being in the comforts of our residences. Our active engagement, motivation and persistence as a team has delivered a fruitful outcome associated with a plethora of experiences and learnings. We have learned that if we imagine and hope, we can indeed achieve astounding results.
We, as a team, have grown to accept the new normal and strive to make the framework more user-friendly.
