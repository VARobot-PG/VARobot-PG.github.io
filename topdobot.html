Dobot Magician

The Dobot is a robotic arm. It is an industrial robot that performs the function of picking up objects. The ability of the Dobot to pick up objects is dependent on the proximity of the objects to the Dobot because the Dobot is immobile.
However, the use of the Dobot is not limited to the industries. It has proven to be useful for educational purposes like robot training, workshop, and in high school. The Dobot also acts as support for robots that are unable to perform the function of object lifting.

This compact, educational robotic arm contains many interesting attributes. Here are the technical specifications of the DOBOT Magician:


SPECIFICATIONS

  • Number of Axis: 4
  • Payload: 500g
  • Max. Reach: 320mm
  • Position Repeatability (Control): 0.2 mm
  • Connectivity: USB / WIFI / Bluetooth
  • Power Supply: 100 V – 240 V , 50/60 HZ
  • Power In: 12 V / 7A DC
  • Consumption: 60W Max
  • Working Temperature: -10°C – 60°C

AXIS MOVEMENT

  • Joint 1 base – Range: -90° to + 90° / Max. Speed: 320° per second
  • Joint 2 rear arm – Range: 0° to +85° / Max. Speed: 320° per second
  • Joint 3 forearm – Range: -10° to +95° / Max. Speed: 320° per second
  • Joint 4 rotation servo – Range: +90° to -90° / Max. Speed: 480° per

PHYSICAL

  • Net Weight: 3.4KG
  • Gross weight (Standard Version): 7.2KG
  • Gross weight (Education Version): 8.0KG
  • Base Dimension (Footprint): 158mm × 158mm
  • Materials: Aluminum Alloy 6061, ABS Engineering Plastic
  • Controller: Dobot Integrated Controller
  • Robot Mounting: Desktop
  • Packing Size: 330mm x 325mm x 410mm
  • Carton Size for Standard Version: 380mm x 385mm x 480mm
  • Carton Size for Education Version: 380mm x 385mm x 480mm

APPLICATIONS

  • Software: DobotStudio, Repetier Host, GrblController3.6, DobotBlockly
  • SDK: Communication Protocol, Dobot Program Library

ENDEFFECTORS

  • 3D Printer Kit

  • Pen Holder
